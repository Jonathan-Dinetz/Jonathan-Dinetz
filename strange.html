Here is a selection of some failed and unfounded work that has piled up in recent months. In the first project, I used a moire effect to create boxes that produce analog animations powered by a viewer’s change in position. Next: some balloon experiments. I used a vinyl cutter to print on balloon. I also experimented with laying out balloons with fishing wire so that, floating, they line up and make one shape ("no"), and when they lose their helium lift, they make another: a heart (don't read into it). Finally, the letter "R" made of tubes. Inspired by a type specimen I found at Providence Public Library, the idea was to spray paint a ball with glow in the dark pigment and shoot it through the tube circuit fast enough to make the "R" glow continuously. I was frighteningly close. Stay tuned.
